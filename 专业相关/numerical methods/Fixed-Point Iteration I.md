## Existence of a Fixed Point
 If $g \in C[a,b]$ and $g(x) \in [a,b]$ for all $x \in [a,b]$ then the function g has a fixed point in $[a,b]$
## Uniqueness Result
Let $g \in C[a,b]$ and $g(x) \in [a,b]$ for all $x \in [a,b]$. Further if g'(x) exists on (a,b) and $$|g'(x)| \le k <1, \forall x \in [a,b],$$ then the function g has a unique fixed point p in $[a,b]$
